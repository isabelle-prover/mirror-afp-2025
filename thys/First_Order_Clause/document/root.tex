\begin{abstract}
 This entry provides reusable theories that lift properties of first-order (ground and nonground) terms to atoms, literals, and clauses. These properties include substitutions, orders, entailment, and typing. The sessions \verb!AFP/First_Order_Terms! and \verb!AFP/Abstract_Substitution! are the basis of this entry.
\end{abstract}
